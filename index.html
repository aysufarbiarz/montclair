Key Terms & Context

Funding Gap ($5M): The difference between what the schools need to spend to maintain current services ($159.2M) and the expected revenue ($154.2M).
Advance of State Aid (Vote No): This is essentially a **loan** from the State of New Jersey. While it covers the $5M gap now, it must be repaid with interest in future budget cycles, which may lead to further cuts later.
Tax Levy (Vote Yes): This is a **permanent increase** in the local tax rate. It covers the $5M gap by increasing the baseline revenue, avoiding the need for a state loan and future interest payments.
Mid-year Cuts ($2.8M): These represent potential reductions in staff, programs, or resources that may be required during the school year if the budget is not fully balanced.
