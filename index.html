Key Terms & Context

Funding Gap ($5M): The difference between projected expenditures ($159.2M) and expected revenue ($154.2M).
Advance of State Aid (Vote No): A loan from the State. It covers the $5M gap now but must be repaid with interest, potentially impacting future budgets.
Tax Levy (Vote Yes): A permanent increase in local tax revenue to cover the $5M gap directly, avoiding loans and interest.
Mid-year Cuts ($2.8M): Reductions required to balance the budget if existing revenue doesn't meet costs.
